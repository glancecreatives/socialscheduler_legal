Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how SocialScheduler ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information (such as name and email)
  • TikTok account data (authorized via OAuth)
  • Content data (videos, captions, scheduling preferences)
  • Usage data (interactions with the platform)

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide content scheduling and publishing features
  • Allow users to connect and manage their TikTok accounts
  • Improve platform performance and user experience

3. TikTok Integration

SocialScheduler uses official TikTok authorization (OAuth). We do not access your TikTok account without your explicit permission.

We only perform actions (such as publishing content) based on user instructions.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share data with service providers only as necessary to operate the platform.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect user data from unauthorized access or misuse.

6. User Control

Users have full control over their connected TikTok accounts, scheduled content, and stored data.

7. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide our services.

8. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time.
