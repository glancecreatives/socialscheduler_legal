Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 13, 2025

Social Scheduler (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect your TikTok and Instagram account information (such as user ID and access token) only after you explicitly authorize our app via each platform’s official OAuth process. We do not collect or store your personal data beyond what is required to upload videos on your behalf.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your access tokens are used solely to upload videos, generate captions, and schedule posts according to your authorization. We do not share or sell this information to any third party.

3. Data Storage

We do not permanently store any user-generated content or access tokens. Tokens are stored securely in temporary memory only for the duration of your session or scheduled task.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with TikTok and Instagram APIs to publish videos on your behalf. All API interactions comply with TikTok Developer Terms of Service and Meta Platform Policies.

5. Your Rights

You can revoke app access at any time through your TikTok or Instagram account settings. Once revoked, we no longer have permission to access or post to your accounts.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@socialscheduler.app.
